Attempting to Embed Full Google Maps Website

Test 1: Full Website (www.google.com/maps) - WILL FAIL ❌

Test 2: Legacy Embed (maps.google.com with output=embed) - WORKS ✅

Why Test 1 Fails:

Google sets X-Frame-Options: SAMEORIGIN on www.google.com/maps which prevents embedding in iframes from other domains.

Open your browser console (F12) to see the error message.
